Chapter One

Places That Became Ours

The places were never special because of where they were. They became special because you were there.

✈️

Airport

You told me you wouldn't be able to make it until a couple of hours later, and I believed you. Then you showed up anyway and surprised me. I don't think you realize how happy that made me. Every love story starts somewhere, and I'm so grateful this chapter started with you waiting there.

🚗

Dmart Parking Lot

To anyone else, it was just two people sitting in a parked car. To me, it became my favorite memory. Nothing huge happened. We were just sitting close, talking about everything and nothing, and existing together. That's when I realized I don't need fancy dates to be happy. I just need you.

🎬

PVR Cinema

The movies were never the best part. The second time we went, the movie was loud, but somehow it felt like we were in our own little world. We were so into our talks, our laughs, and our little moments that the screen almost disappeared. I think that's what you do to places. You make them feel like us.

🌅

PDPU Bridge

That sunrise will always have a special place in my heart. Not just because the view was beautiful, but because I got to experience it with you. Whenever I think of that bridge, I don't think about the road or the sky first. I think about us, standing there while the world was waking up.

🏡

Your Home

The first time I came over wasn't very long, but even those few minutes became a memory I'll always smile about. It wasn't the house that mattered. It was the feeling of being close to you. Your home became another place that reminds me of comfort, laughter, and you.

🏡

My Home

You came over, and suddenly a place I'd known my whole life felt different. Warmer. Happier. Like one of my favorite people had become part of one of my favorite places. Every time I think about it, I remember how natural it felt to simply have you there.

Coming Soon...

This card is intentionally empty because my favorite place hasn't been built yet. One day, I hope this page simply says: Our Home.

If we had different names, different cities, different lives...

Some things would never change.

I'd still think your smile is my favorite. I'd still be proud of you. I'd still believe in you. I'd still choose you.

Thank you for becoming my peace. Thank you for making ordinary places become my favorite memories.

If we met again, I'd still walk toward you. I'd still start that first conversation. I'd still wait for your messages. I'd still fall in love with you. I'd still choose this life.

Because my favorite part of this story was never where it happened. It was always who I lived it with.

I'd spend it looking for you all over again.
